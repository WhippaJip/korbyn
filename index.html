surface
Korbyn is a shell of the kind and compassionate person he used to be. Time and painful experiences have made him into what he is now. His gentle traits quickly changed into insensitive remarks, his true feelings shut off in fear of feeling anything close to affection towards anyone. Most often he is reserved and quiet; keeping to himself is the safest way to go. once his feelings have accumulated over a certain amount of time he is known to have an outburst, shortly followed by isolation. Any act of kindness or empathy is done in such a manner that nothing can be directed back towards him--he hates being acknowledged.
deeper
After enough alone time, if silence occurs he will try his best to strike a conversation in hopes of detering awkwardness. The closer Korbyn becomes to a person the more likely he is to have an outburst; he quit eliterally cannot handle the thought of becoming attached. However, when all is said and done he finds himself observing more details about a person's well being after he has become attached.
deepest
Once Korbyn has become completely attached to someone, his emotions become a lot more unstable. He is prone to have emotional conflictions within his conscience, but once the bond is made his want for a deeper relationship overpowers his fear. As a result of this, he becomes extremely attached, not wanting to leave this person's side in fear of what he might now lose. In addition, he becomes very concerned for their well-being and happiness.
romantic
This resembles his deepest level of friendship, but differs in a few ways. When he becomes attached to a person of romantic interest he becomes very attached. His selfish traits quickly bloom into a very petty jealousy. He will also become possessive and more stubborn than normal. His compassion will show its colors quietly and his gentleness will become unpredictable.
