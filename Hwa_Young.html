Hwa's appearance is most often hidden under a barrage of baggy clothing and ball caps, but if you were to catch her in normal standards you would notice that she is quite thin. As a result of being shielded from the sun for so long, her skin is a creamy pale. If she were exposed to the sun more she would have scattered freckles across her nose and cheeks. She stands at an average height of 5'3, but can be caught wearing combat boots that make her appear taller. Her upper body is rather toned, but her lower half appears coltish.

Her hair is kept at a short length just below her ears and above the collarbone. This way she can enjoy having feminine hair while still being able to tuck it into a cap. The color of her hair has varied through the years, but she currently sports a natural, light brown color. Similar to her hair, Hwa's eyes are a striking amber, most often hidden under her baseball cap.

Much to her surprise, Young has only a few notable scars. The first scar you would most likely notice would be the squiggle of a circle that traces around her thumb joint. The second would be a deep scar in her left bicep. And lastly, a scar across her abdomen, stretching from one side of her belly to the other.
